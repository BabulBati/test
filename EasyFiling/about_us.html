Registered agent

Every state requires a corporation or an LLC to have a Registered Agent. If needed, EasyFiling can provide you with a Registered Agent as a part of all plans, and any official legal and tax correspondence from the state will be digitized and available for access in your dashboard.

EIN service

An Employer Identification Number (EIN), also known as a Tax ID Number, is a unique 9-digit code assigned by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to identify your business. It serves as a digital fingerprint for your company, similar to a social security number for individuals.

ITIN service

Using EasyFiling, one can get an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN), a unique identification number supplied by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). This number is only for those who are required to file taxes in the United States but are unable to obtain a Social Security number.

US bank account

One of the initial steps to undertake following the establishment of a business is to create a dedicated business bank account. This action serves to segregate personal and business finances, reducing personal liability. Additionally, it aids in streamlining tax procedures.
