1
Sign in to KrispCall

Access your KrispCall account to begin the integration process with HubSpot.

2
Navigate to Integration Settings

Go to the settings menu, find the integration option and select HubSpot to start.

3
Select & Add HubSpot Account to KrispCall

Choose the specific HubSpot account you want to integrate & click the toggle button to activate HubSpot integration with KrispCall.

4
Access Integration Settings

Click on the three-dot icon next to HubSpot and select "Edit" to configure the integration.

5
Customize Integration Settings

Adjust the general workflow, connected numbers, and advanced settings as needed for seamless operation.
